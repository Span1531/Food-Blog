Korean Cuisine, Jin's Favourite

Welcome to the Korean Cuisine page! Korean cuisine is characterized by bold flavors, vibrant colors, and a balance of spicy, sweet, sour, and savory tastes. Fermented foods like kimchi play a significant role, as do various meats and vegetables. Some iconic dishes include Kimchi, Hobak Buchim, Dubu Jorim, Oi Muchim, Tofu Gimbap, Kongnamul Japchae, and Miyeok Muchim.
