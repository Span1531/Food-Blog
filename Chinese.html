Chinese Cuisine, Li's Favourite

Welcome to the Chinese Cuisine page! Chinese cuisine is known for its balance of taste, aroma, and color. From the spicy Sichuan dishes to the delicate flavors of Cantonese cooking, Chinese food is beloved around the world. Common ingredients include soy sauce, ginger, garlic, and a variety of vegetables. Signature dishes include Chow Mein, Fried Rice, Scallion buns, Spring rolls, Hot-Pot, Garlic cucumber salad, and Dumplings.
