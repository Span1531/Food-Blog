North Indian Cuisine

Welcome to the North Indian Cuisine page! North Indian cuisine is known for its rich flavors, aromatic spices, and a wide variety of dishes. Popular in regions like Punjab, Uttar Pradesh, and Rajasthan, the cuisine is famous for its use of dairy products, including paneer, and its bread varieties like naan, paratha, and roti. Some of the signature dishes include Palak panner, Butter Naan, Shahi Panner, Butter Masala, Panner Chatpat, and Dal fry.
